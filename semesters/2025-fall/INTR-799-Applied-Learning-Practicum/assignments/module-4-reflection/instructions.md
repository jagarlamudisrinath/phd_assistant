## Assignment Overview

Nearing the end of the semester, it is now time for students to reflect on the knowledge obtained in their course(s) and determine the effectiveness of incorporating real-world experience into our academic curriculum.

## Learning Objectives

Students should:
- Be able to apply knowledge and theory gained in their courses of study within their current workplace or in their future employment
- Be able to demonstrate the application of theory to the workplace in written form
- Be able to identify the benefits of incorporating real-world experience into an academic program

## Assignment Requirements

Students will reflect on the connection between knowledge concepts for courses they are enrolled for the current semester and how those have been, or could be, applied in the workplace.

### Part 1: Course Information

**What courses did you take this semester? Provide course ID and course name.**

List all courses you are enrolled in this semester with their course codes and full names.

### Part 2: Reflection on Knowledge Application

**Reflect on the connection between knowledge concepts from the above listed courses and how those have been, or could be, applied in the workplace.**

Write a reflection that demonstrates how the concepts, theories, and knowledge from your current semester courses:
- Have been applied in your current workplace, OR
- Could be applied in your future employment

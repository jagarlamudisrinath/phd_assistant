# Journal Article Review - Grading Rubric

**Course:** DSRT 734 - Inferential Statistics in Decision-making

**Total Points:** 300 (100%)

---

## Grading Criteria

### 1. Understanding of Research Article
**Weight:** 25% of total grade (75 points)

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Exceptional** | The submission demonstrates a deep understanding of the research article, thoroughly discussing the context, key concepts, and previous weaknesses with insight. | 75 |
| **Proficient** | The submission shows a solid understanding of the research article, addressing the context and key concepts adequately, with minor details missing. | 56.25 |
| **Basic** | The submission indicates a basic understanding of the research article, touching on some context and key concepts but lacking depth and clarity. | 37.5 |
| **Needs Improvement** | The submission shows little to no understanding of the research article, with critical gaps in context and key concepts. | 18.75 |

---

### 2. Methodology Description
**Weight:** 20% of total grade (60 points)

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Exceptional** | The methodology is described in detail, clearly explaining data collection and analysis methods with strong clarity on the research questions and statistical analyses used. | 60 |
| **Proficient** | The methodology is described adequately, with a good explanation of data collection and analysis methods, though some details may be vague. | 45 |
| **Basic** | The methodology is minimally described, with limited explanation of data collection and research questions; lacks clarity in the statistical analysis. | 30 |
| **Needs Improvement** | The methodology is poorly described or missing, with very few details on data collection, analysis methods, or research questions. | 15 |

---

### 3. Analysis of Findings
**Weight:** 25% of total grade (75 points)

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Exceptional** | The analysis of findings is comprehensive, highlighting all major results, limitations, and providing critical insight into their significance. | 75 |
| **Proficient** | The analysis of findings is complete, discussing all major results and limitations, but may lack some critical evaluation. | 56.25 |
| **Basic** | The analysis of findings is basic, addressing some results and limitations, but lacking thoroughness and depth. | 37.5 |
| **Needs Improvement** | The analysis of findings is unclear or missing, failing to address major results and limitations of the study. | 18.75 |

---

### 4. Conclusion Evaluation
**Weight:** 20% of total grade (60 points)

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Exceptional** | The evaluation of conclusions is insightful and analytical, discussing the significance, methods, readability, and implications of the results fully. | 60 |
| **Proficient** | The evaluation of conclusions covers most aspects adequately, addressing significance and implications but with less depth. | 45 |
| **Basic** | The evaluation of conclusions is basic, mentioning significance and implications but lacking detail and depth. | 30 |
| **Needs Improvement** | The evaluation of conclusions lacks clarity or is missing, providing minimal insight into the significance or implications. | 15 |

---

### 5. APA Formatting and Referencing
**Weight:** 10% of total grade (30 points)

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Exceptional** | The submission follows APA formatting perfectly, with all references correctly cited and formatted according to the latest guidelines. | 30 |
| **Proficient** | The submission follows APA formatting well, with most references correctly cited and minor errors in formatting. | 22.5 |
| **Basic** | The submission has some APA formatting issues and several citation errors, demonstrating a basic understanding of the guidelines. | 15 |
| **Needs Improvement** | The submission does not adhere to APA formatting, with major errors in citations and reference list missing or incorrect. | 7.5 |

---

## Total Grade Calculation

| Criterion | Weight | Points |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Understanding of Research Article | 25% | 75 |
| Methodology Description | 20% | 60 |
| Analysis of Findings | 25% | 75 |
| Conclusion Evaluation | 20% | 60 |
| APA Formatting and Referencing | 10% | 30 |
| **TOTAL** | **100%** | **300** |

---

## Note

This rubric applies to any assignment in this course that requires journal article review or critique.

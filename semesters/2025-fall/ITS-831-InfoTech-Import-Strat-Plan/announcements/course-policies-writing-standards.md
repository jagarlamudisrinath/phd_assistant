## Original Work and AI Usage

Please ensure that you are submitting **original work**.

### Acceptable AI Use:
- Help finding sources
- Checking grammar and APA style

### Issues That Will Result in Points Deducted:
- Overly-bulleted papers
- Lack of in-text citations
- Oddly bolded words in your papers (**no words should be bolded in the papers**)
- Lack of properly-formatted references or structure
- Papers not double-spaced or otherwise formatted to APA-style

**Important**: Overly-bulleted papers and the lack of in-text citations are **more than just formatting issues**. They weaken your critical analysis and source application.

## Dissertation Preparation - Writing Standards

As my own Dissertation Chair informed me:

> **"A PhD has no opinion for which he or she cannot cite a source."**

### Writing Rules:
1. **Avoid stating opinions** or the use of **personal pronouns** in your papers (Discussions are OK)
2. Opinions need to be supported by **peer-reviewed sources**
3. Personal pronouns such as "I" or "my" are just grandiose-sounding
4. Avoid "Let's" statements (as in "Let's imagine...")
5. Papers/case studies should be **more formal in tone**
6. Papers are not "collaborative" efforts where we want reader participation

### Why These Standards Matter:
You need to start preparing for the most significant academic paper you'll ever do - **your Dissertation** - so I do grade on these elements to get you into good practices now for that time.

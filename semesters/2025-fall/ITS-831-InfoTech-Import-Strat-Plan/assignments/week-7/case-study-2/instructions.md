## Assignment Overview

Businesses across industries are leveraging predictive analytics and digital transformation to remain competitive, improve efficiency, and enhance decision-making. Predictive analytics enables organizations to anticipate market trends, customer behaviors, and operational challenges by utilizing data-driven insights. Similarly, digital transformation allows businesses to integrate emerging technologies like AI, cloud computing, and automation to streamline operations and enhance customer experiences.

## Selected Case Study

**Company**: Rocket Mortgage / Quicken Loans
**Industry**: Mortgage Lending / Financial Services
**Focus**: Digital transformation through predictive analytics, AI-driven underwriting, and fully digital mortgage experience

## Key Points to Address

### Business Background
- Quicken Loans founded 1985
- Traditional mortgage process: 30-45 day approval, paper-intensive
- Challenges: customer friction, regulatory compliance, competition

### Technologies Adopted
- Rocket Mortgage platform (launched 2015)
- AI/ML for credit risk assessment
- Automated document verification (OCR, NLP)
- Real-time data integration for income/employment verification
- Cloud infrastructure

### Outcomes
- 8-minute conditional approval (vs. weeks)
- #1 J.D. Power customer satisfaction (12+ years)
- Largest retail mortgage lender in US
- $320+ billion loan originations

### Lessons
- Customer-centric design
- Data infrastructure investment
- Regulatory compliance as competitive advantage
- Continuous innovation culture

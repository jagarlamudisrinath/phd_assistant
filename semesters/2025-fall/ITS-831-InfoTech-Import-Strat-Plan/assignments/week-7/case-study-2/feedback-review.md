### PRIORITY 2: Fix First-Line Paragraph Indentation ⚠️ QUICK FIX

**Impact**: 2-3 points
**Professor's previous feedback**: Specifically flagged this issue

**How to fix in Word**:
1. Select all body text (Ctrl+A)
2. Right-click → Paragraph
3. Indentation → Special: First line → By: 0.5"
4. Click OK

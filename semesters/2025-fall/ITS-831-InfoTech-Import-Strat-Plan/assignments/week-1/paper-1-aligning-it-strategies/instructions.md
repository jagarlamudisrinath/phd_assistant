# Paper 1: Aligning IT Strategies

**Course:** ITS-831-B02 - InfoTech Import in Strat Plan
**Term:** 2025 Fall - Second Bi-term

---

## Overview

Aligning IT strategies with business goals is a critical function of modern organizations, ensuring that technology investments drive innovation, efficiency, and competitive advantage. Equally important is the strategic use of information resources, which enables organizations to leverage data, analytics, and digital capabilities to enhance decision-making, optimize operations, and create business value.

---

## Assignment

Develop a paper that addresses strategies for aligning IT strategies with business goals and the role of information resources in supporting this alignment.

### Research Questions to Address:

1. **What does it mean to align IT strategies with business goals**, and why is this alignment essential for organizational success?

2. **How does misalignment between IT and business strategy impact organizational performance?**

3. **How can organizations strategically use information resources** to drive business intelligence, competitive advantage, and operational efficiency?

4. **What strategies can IT leaders implement to proactively align IT with business objectives?**

5. **How can IT leaders work collaboratively with business executives** to ensure alignment while maximizing the value of information resources?

## Assignment Instructions

### Initial Post Requirements

Answer the following questions about IT Project Management. Utilize the UC library for information and examples:

1. **What is a project, and what are its main attributes?**
2. **How is a project different from what most people do in their day-to-day jobs?**
3. **Discuss the importance of top management commitment and the development of standards for successful project management.**
4. **Provide examples to illustrate the importance of these items based on your experience on any type of project.**
5. **Discuss the unique challenges that an IT project presents.**

### Peer Response Requirements

Respond to **two peers** with substantive responses that:
- Discuss your thoughts on project management
- Share your views on project attributes
- Discuss your thoughts on successful project management
- You can take opposing/differing views than your peers but provide applicable resources
- Provide examples in your peer responses
- Discuss any additional challenges you see with IT projects

A substantive post will do **at least two** of the following:
- Ask an interesting, thoughtful question pertaining to the topic
- Answer a question (in detail) posted by another student or the instructor
- Share an applicable personal experience

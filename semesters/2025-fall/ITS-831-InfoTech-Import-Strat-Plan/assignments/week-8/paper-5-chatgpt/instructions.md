## Context

Generative AI, such as ChatGPT, has rapidly integrated into various aspects of professional and academic writing, raising profound implications for education, research, and intellectual integrity. Chapter 13 explores how generative AI can produce not only text-based content, such as resumes and cover letters, but also fabricated yet highly realistic images and persuasive essays. However, despite its capabilities, concerns persist regarding its reliability, particularly with "hallucinated" references and the ethical implications of using AI-generated content in academic and professional settings.

At the PhD level, the challenge is not merely assessing AI's output for superficial similarities but critically engaging with its epistemological, ethical, and methodological ramifications. Therefore, this assignment requires a deeper investigation into the role of generative AI in scholarly work.

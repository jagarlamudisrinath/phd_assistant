## Assignment Description

Develop a scholarly paper that critically examines the practice of Information Systems (IS) outsourcing. Your analysis should explore both the challenges and advantages associated with outsourcing IS functions, considering factors such as:

- Cost efficiency
- Access to specialized expertise
- Security risks
- Potential loss of control over critical business functions

Additionally, discuss the strategic implications of outsourcing in the context of business competitiveness and operational efficiency. To support your discussion, provide a detailed case study of a company that has successfully leveraged outsourcing as part of its IS strategy to achieve a competitive advantage.

## Suggested Paper Structure

### 1. Introduction (0.5-1 page)
- Define IS outsourcing and its prevalence in modern business
- State the purpose of the paper
- Preview the main topics to be discussed

### 2. Advantages of IS Outsourcing (1-1.5 pages)
- Cost efficiency and financial benefits
- Access to specialized expertise and talent
- Focus on core business competencies
- Scalability and flexibility
- 24/7 operations and global coverage

### 3. Challenges and Risks of IS Outsourcing (1-1.5 pages)
- Security and data privacy concerns
- Loss of control over critical functions
- Quality control issues
- Communication and cultural barriers
- Hidden costs and vendor lock-in
- Intellectual property risks

### 4. Strategic Implications (0.5-1 page)
- Impact on business competitiveness
- Operational efficiency considerations
- Make-or-buy decision framework
- Long-term strategic alignment

### 5. Case Study (1-1.5 pages)
- Company background and context
- Outsourcing decision and strategy
- Implementation approach
- Results and competitive advantage achieved
- Lessons learned

### 6. Conclusion (0.5 page)
- Summary of key findings
- Recommendations for organizations considering IS outsourcing
- Final thoughts on strategic value

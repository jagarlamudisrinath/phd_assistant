## Discussion Topic

Select an emerging technology (e.g. AI-driven chatbots, blockchain, augmented reality, digital twins, generative AI, quantum computing, etc.) and develop a **3-minute video pitch** addressing:

### Required Components

1. **How the technology can be applied in a specific industry**
   - Choose a specific industry context
   - Explain the practical application

2. **What innovation or operational improvement it brings**
   - Identify specific innovations
   - Describe operational improvements

3. **How it can create or sustain a competitive advantage**
   - Explain the strategic advantage
   - Discuss sustainability of the advantage

4. **Potential risks or barriers to adoption**
   - Identify implementation challenges
   - Discuss risk factors

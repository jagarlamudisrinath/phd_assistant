## Assignment Overview

Established companies face continuous threats from emerging market disruptors that leverage technology, innovation, and alternative business models to challenge incumbents. This assignment requires you to critically analyze a real-world business and assess how it responded to a specific disruptive market threat by strategically leveraging information technology (IT) capabilities.

### Example Context

Walmart faced disruption when competitor Amazon Go began implementing checkout-free stores. The technology opportunity Walmart could evaluate included investment in:
- IoT sensors
- AI-driven inventory management
- Mobile payment platforms

---

## Assignment Requirements

### Task

Find an **actual company** to report on and address how that company responded to a significant market disruption.

In your paper, provide:
1. A **detailed summary** of the industry or company and the disruption
2. Analysis addressing the applicable questions listed below

---

## Key Analysis Questions

Address the following questions **as applicable to the case under review**:

### 1. Early Warning Signs and Competitive Response
- What were the key signals or early warning signs of the market disruption?
- How did competitors or industry leaders respond?

### 2. External Factors
- What role did external factors play in accelerating or mitigating the disruption?
  - Economic trends
  - Regulations
  - Consumer preferences
  - Supply chain disruptions

### 3. Technology's Role
- What specific role did AI, automation, or cloud computing play (or could have played) in the company's response?

### 4. Barriers to Effective Response
- Were there any major barriers that prevented the company from responding more effectively?
  - Organizational barriers
  - Financial barriers
  - Technological barriers

### 5. Anticipation and Adaptation
- How could the company have better anticipated and adapted to the market disruption?

### 6. Balancing Innovation and Cannibalization
- How can companies balance the potential cannibalization of existing products and services with the need to innovate?

### 7. Strategic Lessons
- What lessons can companies learn from this company's experience regarding aligning technology adoption with organizational strategy?

### 8. Future Disruptions
- What future disruptions or technological shifts could further impact the company or industry?

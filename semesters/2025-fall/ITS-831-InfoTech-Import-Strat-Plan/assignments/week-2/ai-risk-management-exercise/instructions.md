### 2. Identify an AI System

**Selection:**
- Research and choose (or make up) an AI system you are familiar with OR
- Provide a case study of an AI system used in a real-world scenario (e.g., facial recognition system, AI-based financial advisor, autonomous vehicle)
- **Simple scenarios are provided in Appendix A** (below)
- You are encouraged to develop more complex scenarios

**Description:**
- Write a description of the AI system, including:
  - Its purpose
  - Functionality
  - Potential stakeholders

### 3. Risk Identification

**Activity:**
- Identify potential risks associated with the selected AI system using risk taxonomies
- Consider:
  - Technical risks
  - Ethical risks
  - Operational risks
  - Legal risks

**Resources:**
- The **[DOE AI Risk Management Playbook (AIRMP)](https://web.archive.org/web/20240318152252/https://www.energy.gov/ai/doe-ai-risk-management-playbook-airmp)** provides information on AI-specific risks and associated controls
- You can use this or other resources as examples of associated AI risks
- **Make sure your risks are specific to the AI company**

**Documentation:**
- Document the identified risks in the risk register template (see attached Excel template: `AI Risk Register Template.xlsx`)

### 4. Risk Analysis and Evaluation

**Activity:**
- Assess the **likelihood** and **impact** of each identified risk using qualitative and/or quantitative methods
- Assess the risk level

**Prioritization:**
- Use a **risk matrix** to prioritize the risks based on their likelihood and impact
- **Tip:** Rearrange the risks in the register so that they are in order by this prioritization

### 5. Developing Mitigation Strategies

**Activity:**
- For each prioritized risk, develop appropriate mitigation strategies, including:
  - Technical controls
  - Organizational controls
  - Process controls

**Resources to Consider:**
- Best practices information from course materials
- **NIST SP 800-53, Rev.5** for controls
- Other relevant resources

**Documentation:**
- Document the mitigation strategies in the risk register

### 6. Continuous Monitoring and Reporting

**Activity:**
- Outline a plan for continuous monitoring of the AI system
- Define reporting mechanisms to ensure ongoing risk management
- **Important:** Identify metrics that will allow you to monitor effectiveness

**Documentation:**
- Include the monitoring and reporting plan in the risk register

## APPENDIX A: Scenarios

You may choose one of these scenarios or develop your own:

### 1. Retail Corporation: Facial Recognition Security System

**Business Description:**

A large retail corporation with hundreds of stores nationwide has implemented a facial recognition system at its entry points. The system is designed to identify known shoplifters and alert security staff in real-time, enhancing in-store security and deterring theft.

The company claims this system reduces losses from theft by 20%, a significant impact on its bottom line and, as such, is highly critical to store security.

**AI System Description:**

The facial recognition system is a cloud-based AI solution that uses real-time video feeds to match faces against a database of known offenders. The system stores facial data, making it essential for security, but also raises privacy and data security concerns.

---

### 2. Healthcare Provider: AI Diagnosis Assistance System

**Business Description:**

A mid-sized healthcare provider uses an AI diagnostic system to assist radiologists in identifying early signs of cancer. This system helps improve diagnostic accuracy and speed, providing essential support to healthcare professionals in life-critical cases.

The system is critical for patient care and the healthcare provider's credibility. It enhances the speed and accuracy of diagnoses, potentially reducing treatment costs and improving patient outcomes.

**AI System Description:**

The AI is trained on millions of radiology images and provides predictions on potential cancerous growths in X-rays and MRIs. It highlights areas of concern for further review by radiologists, helping them focus on high-risk cases quickly.

---

### 3. Financial Institution: AI-Powered Loan Approval System

**Business Description:**

A financial institution has integrated an AI system into its loan approval process, helping automate credit assessments and predict loan default risks. The system promises to streamline approvals, reduce processing time, and improve loan portfolio quality by more accurately assessing applicants' creditworthiness.

This system is moderately to highly critical, as it impacts loan approval processes, revenue, and regulatory compliance.

**AI System Description:**

The AI analyzes applicant data, including credit history, income levels, and spending habits, to score potential risks and make loan recommendations. This system is critical for ensuring fair, data-driven decisions in loan approvals and minimizing default rates.

---

### 4. Government Civilian Agency: Citizen Support Chatbot

**Business Description:**

A government civilian agency has deployed an AI chatbot to assist citizens with frequently asked questions and provide general support for accessing services online. The chatbot operates 24/7, providing a faster response time and reducing the workload of human staff.

This system is moderately critical, as it enhances accessibility and service efficiency, especially during peak hours. However, it is less critical than human agents for complex queries.

**AI System Description:**

The chatbot uses natural language processing (NLP) to understand and respond to user inquiries on the agency's website. It handles basic questions related to applications and document requirements.

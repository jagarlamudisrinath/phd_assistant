# Paper Grading Rubric

**Course:** ITS-831-B02 - InfoTech Import in Strat Plan

**Total Points:** 75 (100%)

---

## Grading Criteria

### 1. Content
**Weight:** 25% of total grade

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Meets Expectations** | Demonstrates strong or adequate knowledge of the materials; correctly represents knowledge from the readings and sources. | 90-100% |
| **Approaches Expectations** | A few errors or omissions in demonstration of knowledge. | 80-89% |
| **Below Expectations** | Some significant but not major errors or omissions in demonstration of knowledge. | 70-79% |
| **No Evidence** | Fails to provide enough content to show a demonstration of knowledge. | 0-69% |

---

### 2. Critical Analysis
**Weight:** 30% of total grade

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Meets Expectations** | Provides a strong critical analysis and interpretation of the information given. | 90-100% |
| **Approaches Expectations** | A few errors or omissions in analysis and interpretation. | 80-89% |
| **Below Expectations** | Some significant but not major errors or omissions in analysis and interpretation. | 70-79% |
| **No Evidence** | Fails to provide a critical thinking analysis and interpretation. | 0-69% |

---

### 3. Problem Solving
**Weight:** 25% of total grade

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Meets Expectations** | Demonstrates strong or adequate thought and insight in problem solving. | 90-100% |
| **Approaches Expectations** | A few errors or omissions in problem solving. | 80-89% |
| **Below Expectations** | Some significant but not major errors or omissions in problem solving. | 70-79% |
| **No Evidence** | Fails to demonstrate problem solving. | 0-69% |

---

### 4. Sources/Examples
**Weight:** 10% of total grade

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Meets Expectations** | Sources or examples meet required criteria and are well chosen to provide substance and perspectives on the issue under examination. | 90-100% |
| **Approaches Expectations** | Sources or examples meet required criteria but are less than adequately chosen to provide substance and perspectives on the issue under examination. | 80-89% |
| **Below Expectations** | Sources or examples meet required criteria but are less than adequately chosen to provide substance and perspectives on the issue under examination. | 70-79% |
| **No Evidence** | Source or example selection and integration of knowledge from the course is clearly deficient. | 0-69% |

---

### 5. Organization, Grammar, Style
**Weight:** 5% of total grade

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Meets Expectations** | Demonstrates strong or adequate thought and insight in problem solving. | 90-100% |
| **Approaches Expectations** | Project is fairly well organized and written, and is in proper format as outlined in the assignment. Reasonably good sentence and paragraph structure; significant number of errors in grammar and spelling. | 80-89% |
| **Below Expectations** | Project is adequately organized and written, and is in proper format as outlined in the assignment. Reasonably good sentence and paragraph structure; significant number of errors in grammar and spelling. | 70-79% |
| **No Evidence** | Project is not organized or well written, and is not in proper paper format. Poor-quality work; unacceptable in terms of grammar and spelling. | 0-69% |

---

### 6. Proper Use of APA Formatting
**Weight:** 5% of total grade

| Performance Level | Description | Points |
|------------------|-------------|--------|
| **Meets Expectations** | Sources or examples meet required criteria and are well chosen to provide substance and perspectives on the issue under examination. | 90-100% |
| **Approaches Expectations** | Sources or examples meet required criteria but are less than adequately chosen to provide substance and perspectives on the issue under examination. | 80-89% |
| **Below Expectations** | Significant errors in APA formatting, with four to five significant errors. | 70-79% |
| **No Evidence** | Numerous errors in APA formatting, with more than eight significant errors. | 0-69% |

---

## Total Grade Calculation

| Criterion | Weight | Maximum Points |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Content | 25% | 18.75 |
| Critical Analysis | 30% | 22.5 |
| Problem Solving | 25% | 18.75 |
| Sources/Examples | 10% | 7.5 |
| Organization, Grammar, Style | 5% | 3.75 |
| Proper Use of APA Formatting | 5% | 3.75 |
| **TOTAL** | **100%** | **75** |

---

## Note

This rubric applies to all paper assignments in this course unless otherwise specified.
